About Us

  • Fluke Games
    Fluke Games, a registered trademark in India since 2020, began as a personal portfolio project. Our journey started with deploying a few games to online stores, crafted entirely by one person as a testament to individual creativity and skill. These projects were a showcase of passion and dedication, serving as stepping stones into the expansive gaming industry.

    By 2022, Fluke Games evolved into something more. After starting work on an ambitious project and sharing the experience with peers in the gaming industry, the overwhelmingly positive feedback inspired us to pursue a polished, professional product. Since then, Fluke Games has been fueled by determination and collaboration, with contributions and support gathered from like-minded individuals across the internet.

    Fluke Games is not a company in the conventional sense, nor do we aim to compete with established gaming studios. Instead, our mission is to offer a platform where creators can collaborate on a professional, polished project that enhances their portfolios to seek further employment and opportunities.
  • Vision
    Short-Term Vision: To create a polished, 20-minute vertical slice of a Hindu mythology-inspired game, allowing players to immerse themselves in a thrilling and engaging gameplay experience while bootstrapping our intellectual property. This slice will highlight our creative vision, demonstrate the quality of our work, and act as a foundation for future endeavors.

    Long-Term Vision: To establish Fluke Games as a leading creator of AAA-quality games, producing impactful intellectual properties that merge innovation with storytelling. By leveraging the power of technology and imagination, we aim to create iconic games that leave a lasting impression on players and set new benchmarks in the industry.
  • Mission
    To craft immersive gaming experiences that blend ancient legends with cutting-edge technology, delivering polished and engaging products that resonate with players worldwide. We strive to create games that not only entertain but also inspire, bringing myths and stories to life in ways that are both fresh and impactful. With every project, our mission is to redefine what gaming can be and deliver experiences that players will cherish.
  • Our Values
    1️. Empowering Aspiring Creators: Having spent years in the gaming industry, we understand the challenges aspiring creators face when trying to break into the field. A recurring question we encounter is how to land a job in this competitive space. One consistent piece of advice stands out: portfolio matters. A strong portfolio can open doors, and what better way to build one than by contributing to real-world projects? At Fluke Games, we provide opportunities for individuals to learn, grow, and showcase their abilities in meaningful ways.

    2️. Fostering Collaboration: Gaming is a multidisciplinary industry that thrives on collaboration. To create a polished product, expertise from various artistic and technical domains is crucial, and no single person can do it all. Recognizing this, Fluke Games offers an ideal environment for emerging talent to collaborate, learn, and contribute. We believe that the best results come from a collective effort, where diverse skills and ideas come together to create something extraordinary.

    3️. Building a Creative Community: We believe in the power of community. At Fluke Games, we aim to bring together passionate creators from diverse backgrounds, providing them with opportunities to grow, create, and make a meaningful impact in the gaming industry. By fostering a supportive and inclusive environment, we hope to encourage innovation, build lasting relationships, and inspire the next generation of creators.
  • Our Current Project
    We are currently developing a game inspired by Hanuman, the son of the Wind God, merging ancient legends with modern storytelling and gameplay mechanics. This ambitious project seeks to bring Hindu mythology to life, focusing on Hanuman's iconic abilities, like growing and shrinking, and his legendary Gada. With immersive mechanics and stunning visuals, we aim to honor the richness of the mythology while making it accessible to a global audience.

    The goal is to create a 20-minute playable slice of this game, showcasing thrilling gameplay, fluid mechanics, and immersive storytelling. This slice will act as a proof of concept, representing the game’s quality and our team's dedication to excellence. It will serve as a stepping stone to create a full-fledged game, introducing players to an unforgettable experience steeped in mythology and innovation.
  • Our Approach
    Fluke Games is not a company in the conventional sense, nor do we aim to compete with established gaming studios. Instead, our mission is to offer a platform where creators can collaborate on a professional, polished project that enhances their portfolios. This provides them with a springboard to seek further employment and opportunities within the gaming industry.

    By collaborating in our projects, contributors gain hands-on experience in game development, expand their networks, and create something meaningful that demonstrates their skills to potential employers. Our focus is on building impactful projects while nurturing the next generation of gaming professionals.
